Personal Profile

I am originally from Seattle but I have lived in Utah for the past 8 years. I worked to put myself through school and, while working for a marketing group in SEO, I found that I was more drawn to the technology aspect of my work.

I enrolled in a coding bootcamp through Coding Campus and graduated from both there and BYU at the same time. Almost immediately after graduation, I transfered from my SEO Marketing job and started working with a custom development shop called Lift Media as a combination Web Developer/ Front-end Designer. I work with clients to help them develop and design websites that fit their needs. I think of myself as a developer first with a passion for design (in fact, I enjoyed designing so much that I started my own clothing shop called Wolf & Co where I design & sell my own T-Shirts).

I love creating beautiful and functional websites. If you do too, then we'll make a great team.
